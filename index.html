CORNISH STEAM
Science and Art

Cornish Steam is a unique business that thrives at the intersection of art and science, offering a captivating experience where creativity and innovation intertwine. With a passion for both disciplines, Cornish Steam showcases the beautiful harmony that exists when art and science converge. Through their exceptional craftsmanship and technical expertise, they bring to life mesmerizing steam-powered sculptures and kinetic installations that seamlessly blend aesthetic beauty with scientific principles.

By meticulously fusing these two worlds, Cornish Steam creates a captivating journey for visitors, inviting them to explore the wondrous possibilities when art and science meet. With each creation, they strive to ignite curiosity, inspire imagination, and spark a deeper appreciation for the intrinsic connection between creativity and scientific discovery.
